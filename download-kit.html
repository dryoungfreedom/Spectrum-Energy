Contributing Back

Found something to improve? Discovered a new material or correction? Contributions are welcome through GitHub:

  1. Fork the repository (creates your own copy)
  2. Make your changes — data additions go in spectrum-data.js
  3. Submit a pull request — describe what you changed and why
  4. Review — all submissions are reviewed before merging

Questions? Use the contact form or open an issue on GitHub.
