The Atmospheric Model
Earth's atmosphere filters the sun: gamma/X-ray absorbed high up, UV caught by ozone, only safe bands reach the surface. The SE Cell follows the same architecture.

Inner layers convert dangerous bands. Outer surface is radiologically safe and emits only useful energy. Shielding IS the converter — each layer has dual function: protect AND harvest.
Three Output Modes
⚡Electrical — betavoltaic + CdTe + PV + thermoelectric → wiring, batteries, grid
🔥Direct Thermal — outer shell heat → hydronic loops → home heating, hot water, orchard frost prevention, greenhouses
💡Direct Light — scintillator visible light → fiber optic bundles → interior lighting, workspaces, underground facilities
Design rule: Don't convert energy that's already in a useful form. Every unnecessary conversion step loses energy.
Why All Paths Are Active
In the reactor, beta and Cherenkov compete with the 33% steam cycle. The SE Cell has no steam cycle. Every band starts at 0%. Even 10% betavoltaic and 6% thermoelectric are pure gain.

Electrical paths: β→SiC 10% | γ→CdTe 30% | γ→scint→PV 3.6% | X→CdTe 25% | heat→TE 6%

Direct-use paths: 🔥 heat→hydronic ~95% eff. | 💡 scint→fiber ~80% eff.

Direct use avoids double-conversion losses.
Layer Order = Energy Order
SiC innermost — beta range is millimeters. Must catch it before it becomes heat.

High-Z absorber next — gamma → X-ray downshift via photoelectric effect. 95% re-emitted as characteristic X-rays at lower energy. This is the atmospheric model at work.

CdTe next — catches downshifted X-rays and remaining gamma. Direct → ⚡.

Scintillators outside CdTe — catch lower-E gamma and Compton secondaries → visible light. Some light → PV → ⚡. Some light → fiber → 💡 direct use.

PV wraps scintillators — captures 415nm light emitted in all directions.

Thermal shell outermost — residual heat from all layers. Some → thermoelectric → ⚡. Remainder → hydronic → 🔥 direct use.

Outer surface: Only safe bands remain. Radiologically safe to touch.

Design principles: (1) Harvest from waste, never steal from thermal. (2) Filter by layer, not by containment. (3) Don't convert energy already in useful form. (4) Cascade, don't discard.
